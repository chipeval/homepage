Embedded Vision & AI Lab, KNUT, Korea

Embedded Vision & AI Lab (EVAL) pioneers efficient vision and AI solutions for embedded and edge platforms, bridging advanced algorithms with practical hardware design. At EVAL, we design low-power, high-performance computer vision and AI systems that run seamlessly on chips, devices, and edge environments. EVAL brings together computer vision, AI, and embedded systems to create scalable technologies for real-time, intelligent applications.
